Is a site that improves the method of buying video games, by allowing the user to see all the information about a future videogame purchase, as well as allow them to watch a livestream of that game, so they can confidently waste their money, or shove it back in their pockets. We used Walmart, IGDB, and Twitch's API's to allow us to do this.
